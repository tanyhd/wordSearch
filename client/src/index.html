Word Search !

A word search game is a fun and challenging puzzle game where players have to find a set of words hidden within a grid of letters.

To play the game, you first need to enter a list of words, separated by commas, that you will be searching for in the grid. For example, you might enter "car,dog,mouse" as your list of words.

Next, you will need to enter the size of the grid that you want to generate. For example, you might enter "15" to generate a 15x15 grid.

Once you have entered your list of words and selected the grid size, you can click on the "generate" button to create your word search puzzle.

To play the game, you can click and drag your cursor across the grid to spot the words in the grid. Have fun!
