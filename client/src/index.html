Word Search Puzzle

Puzzle game where players have to find a set of words hidden within a grid of letters.

Enter a list of words, separated by commas, that you will be searching for in the grid. For example: "car,dog,mouse".

Next, enter the size of the grid that you want to generate. For example: "15" to generate a 15x15 grid.

Click on the "Generate Grid" button to create your word search puzzle.

You could also click on "Generate Random Grid" to generate a puzzle with random words and grid size

To play the game, click and drag your cursor across the grid to spot the words in the grid. Have fun!
